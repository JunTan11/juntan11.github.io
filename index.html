About Me

My journey into the fascinating world of programming and technology was carved from a path of diverse learning experiences.

It all intensified during my two-week internship at the Science Centre in 2022. Engaging in research on piezoelectric materials, I was immersed in a rich blend of learning, where theory met application, sparking a deeper interest in STEM.

Serving as the Observatory Director for the Astronomy Club in 2021 to 2022 was a springboard that pushed me to explore the skies and beyond. It inspired self-driven learning, and this quest for knowledge somehow led me into the mesmerizing universe of programming and algorithms.

In 2023, the MOE Engineering and Tech Programme Scholarship (ETPS) marked a significant milestone. It wasn't just an achievement but a catalyst, steering me further into my journey of discovery and learning.
