Creating an AI-Powered Recycling Bin Lid

(SCSE Computing Challenge 2024)

Our project earned my team and I the first place at the SCSE Computing Challenge. Within a month, we developed a sustainable recycling bin lid using a Deep CNN Classifier and Lego robotics for precise sorting into Paper, Plastic, Metal, and Glass. This cost-effective, AI-enhanced lid offers a scalable recycling solution.

About Me

My journey into the fascinating world of STEM was carved from a path of diverse learning experiences.

It all intensified during my two-week internship at the Science Centre in 2022. Engaging in research on piezoelectric materials, I was immersed in a rich blend of learning, sparking a deeper interest in Physics.

Serving as the Observatory Director for the Astronomy Club in 2021 to 2022 was a springboard that pushed me to explore further, leading me into the mesmerizing universe of Astronomy.

In 2023, the MOE Engineering and Tech Programme Scholarship (ETPS) marked a significant milestone. It was a catalyst, steering me further into my journey of discovery in programming and engineering.
